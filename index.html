Trespass Towing Analysis

Alexandra Veremeychik

What is trespass towing?

The towing or removal of a vehicle from private property without the vehicle's owner permission.

 

The vehicle's owner is required to travel to a lot to pay a fee and retrieve their vehicle.

The Process

You park your vehicle

Your vehicle is towed to a storage lot

At the lot you pay a fee to receive vehicle

Property owner authorizes tow in writing

*

 Written authorization is not needed between 2am and 9am or in the case that your vehicle is blocking access

*

Towing company files report to police

The data is collected and stored in a database

Correlations

The number of cars towed from a census tract is more highly correlated with the median household income, language spoken at home, and CEI score than with population or resident density.
